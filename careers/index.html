Electronics Engineer

Zinn Labs is a venture-backed, pre-launch startup on a mission to redefine the role of eyewear in our lives. Spun out of the Stanford Computational Imaging lab, we have developed breakthrough technologies enabling next-generation smart eyewear to offer new interactions, diagnostics, and experiences without sacrificing style. In a TED talk by one of our co-founders, you can learn how our technology can be applied to effectively restore natural vision for anyone over the age of 50.

Zinn Labs is seeking employee #1, an electronics engineer that enjoys prototyping low-power embedded systems and iterating quickly to scale effectively. You will build solutions in a fast-paced and collaborative environment focusing on both low-level software/firmware design and circuit design. You will have an integral role in technical decisions that define our product and influence the customer experience. You will join a collaborative but highly autonomous working environment in which each member has a defined role with clear expectations, as well as the opportunity to pursue projects they find interesting and impactful.

What you’ll do:

  • Rapidly prototype entire embedded system electronics
  • Define and implement product system architecture
  • Design, layout, and test printed circuit boards
  • Bring up firmware
  • Develop and deploy optimized low-level software
  • Contribute to the development of an all-day, every-day eye-sensing platform

What we look for:

  • 5+ years of relevant work experience
  • Experience bringing up embedded systems for low-power, mobile applications
  • PCB CAD experience
  • Embedded system software development experience
  • Experience developing embedded systems firmware for imaging is a plus
  • Experience with FPGA embedded systems and other hardware accelerators is a plus
