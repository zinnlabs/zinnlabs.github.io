Business Development Specialist

Zinn Labs is a venture-backed, pre-launch startup on a mission to redefine the role of eyewear in our lives. Spun out of the Stanford Computational Imaging lab, we have developed breakthrough technologies enabling next-generation smart eyewear to offer new interactions, diagnostics, and experiences without sacrificing style. In a TED talk by one of our co-founders, you can learn how our technology can be applied to effectively restore natural vision for anyone over the age of 50.

Zinn Labs is seeking a full-time business development specialist to expand our business efforts through expert discovery and exploration of new and untapped business opportunities and relationships. As an early business hire you will be expected to wear a number of hats and be quick to learn. You will work closely with the executive and technical teams to develop strategy and launch new programs and products. You will join a collaborative but highly autonomous working environment in which each member has a defined role with clear expectations, as well as the opportunity to pursue projects they find interesting and impactful.

What you’ll do:

  • Take a lead role in the development of proposals and presentations for new business materials to create and nurture business opportunities and partnerships
  • Identify trends and customer needs, building a short/medium/long-term sales pipeline in accordance with targets
  • Enable partner success through relationships management, cross-functional planning, and analytical insights and support
  • Develop strategies and positions by analyzing new venture integration
  • Motivate the team, track performance, and report metrics
  • Contribute to the development of an all-day, every-day eye-sensing platform

What we look for:

  • 5+ years of relevant work experience in a business development position
  • Experience working at an early stage startup
  • Strong communication and interpersonal skills
  • Proven knowledge and execution of successful development strategies
  • Proficiency with data analysis, forecasting, and budgeting
  • Focused and goal-oriented
